Annual Budget

The annual budget outlines the state's financial plan for the fiscal year, including revenue projections and expenditure allocations.

Key Highlights

  • Total budget size: ₹1.2 trillion.
  • Focus on infrastructure development and social welfare.
  • Increased allocation for education and healthcare.

How to Access

The full budget document is available on the official Gujarat Finance Ministry website.

Capital Expenditure

Capital expenditure focuses on long-term investments in infrastructure, such as roads, bridges, and public utilities.

Key Projects

  • Construction of new highways and expressways.
  • Development of smart cities and urban infrastructure.
  • Upgradation of public transportation systems.

How to Access

Detailed project reports are available on the Gujarat Infrastructure Development Portal.

Revenue Expenditure

Revenue expenditure covers operational costs, including salaries, pensions, and maintenance of public services.

Key Areas

  • Education and healthcare services.
  • Public safety and law enforcement.
  • Administrative and operational expenses.

How to Access

Detailed expenditure reports are available on the Gujarat Finance Ministry website.

Taxation

GST

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a comprehensive indirect tax on the supply of goods and services.

Income Tax

Income tax is a direct tax levied on the income of individuals and businesses.

Property Tax

Property tax is a levy on property ownership, used to fund local services.

GST

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a unified tax system that replaces multiple indirect taxes. It is levied at every stage of the supply chain.

Key Features

  • Single tax system for goods and services.
  • Input tax credit mechanism to avoid double taxation.
  • Four-tier tax structure: 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%.

How to File GST

Businesses can file GST returns online through the GST portal.

Income Tax

Income tax is levied on the income of individuals, businesses, and other entities. It is a major source of revenue for the government.

Key Features

  • Progressive tax rates based on income slabs.
  • Deductions and exemptions available for taxpayers.
  • E-filing of income tax returns is mandatory.

How to File Income Tax

Taxpayers can file their income tax returns online through the Income Tax e-Filing portal.

Property Tax

Property tax is a local tax levied on real estate properties. It is used to fund municipal services like roads, sanitation, and public utilities.

Key Features

  • Based on the assessed value of the property.
  • Paid annually or semi-annually.
  • Rebates available for timely payments.

How to Pay Property Tax

Property tax can be paid online through the municipal corporation's website or at designated payment centers.
