PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

The PM Kisan Samman Nidhi scheme provides financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers. The amount is distributed in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Small and marginal farmers with landholding up to 2 hectares.
  • Farmers must be Indian citizens.

How to Apply

Farmers can apply online through the official PM Kisan portal or visit their nearest Common Service Center (CSC).

Soil Health Card

The Soil Health Card scheme provides farmers with a report card on the health of their soil, including nutrient status and recommendations for fertilizers.

Benefits

  • Improves soil fertility and crop productivity.
  • Reduces unnecessary fertilizer usage.

How to Get a Soil Health Card

Farmers can apply through their state agriculture department or visit the Soil Health Card portal.

Crop Insurance

The Crop Insurance scheme provides coverage for crops against natural calamities such as droughts, floods, and pests.

Coverage

  • Protects against yield losses due to natural disasters.
  • Affordable premium rates for farmers.

How to Enroll

Farmers can enroll through their nearest agriculture office or online via the Crop Insurance portal.

Organic Farming

The Organic Farming initiative promotes chemical-free farming practices to ensure sustainable agriculture and healthier crops.

Benefits

  • Improves soil health and fertility.
  • Reduces environmental pollution.
  • Produces healthier and chemical-free food.

How to Participate

Farmers can join the initiative by registering through the state agriculture department or visiting the Organic Farming portal.

Smart Agriculture

The Smart Agriculture initiative uses advanced technology like IoT, drones, and AI to improve farming efficiency and productivity.

Key Features

  • Real-time monitoring of crops and soil conditions.
  • Automated irrigation and pest control systems.
  • Data-driven decision-making for farmers.

How to Enroll

Farmers can enroll through the Smart Agriculture portal or contact their nearest agriculture office.

Water Conservation

The Water Conservation initiative focuses on promoting efficient water usage in agriculture through modern irrigation techniques and awareness programs.

Key Features

  • Drip irrigation and sprinkler systems.
  • Rainwater harvesting and storage solutions.
  • Training programs for farmers on water-efficient practices.

How to Participate

Farmers can participate by attending training sessions or applying for subsidies on water-efficient equipment.
